# Proposed Practice Modes for Spanish 1A Drilling App

This document outlines expanded practice modes designed to complement the core 16 modes (translation and verb conjugation drills between spoken/written English and Spanish). These modes target grammar fluency, syntax, listening comprehension, pronunciation, and rapid recall.

---

## 1. Grammar & Structural Transformations
Instead of translating between English and Spanish, students manipulate Spanish sentences directly to build intuitive grammatical awareness.

### 1.1 Subject / Pronoun Swapping
* **Prompt:** *"Yo **como** la manzana."* (Target Subject: *Nosotros*)
* **Student Response:** *"**Nosotros comemos** la manzana."*
* **Learning Objective:** Reinforces subject-verb agreement without relying on English cues.

### 1.2 Tense & Aspect Conversion
* **Prompt:** *"Ella **habla** con el profesor."* (Convert to Preterite)
* **Student Response:** *"Ella **habló** con el profesor."*
* **Learning Objective:** Trains swift tense switching between core Spanish 1A tenses (Present $\leftrightarrow$ Preterite/Imperfect).

### 1.3 Gender & Number Agreement Drills
* **Prompt:** *"El chico alto"* (Target: Plural Feminine)
* **Student Response:** *"Las chicas altas"*
* **Learning Objective:** Drills article, noun, and adjective agreement across gender and number variations.

### 1.4 Sentence Form Shifts (Affirmative $\leftrightarrow$ Negative / Interrogative)
* **Prompt:** *"Tú quieres café."* (Target: Question or Negative)
* **Student Response:** *"¿Quieres tú café?"* or *"Tú no quieres café."*
* **Learning Objective:** Masters punctuation, intonation, and negative construction patterns.

## 2. Contextual & Syntactic Drills

### 2.1 Sentence Unscramble (Word Order & Pronoun Placement)
* **Prompt:** Scrambled elements `[la]` `[da]` `[nosotros]` `[carta]` `[A]` `[nos]`
* **Student Response:** Reorder into *"A nosotros nos da la carta"*
* **Learning Objective:** Teaches Spanish syntax rules, including direct/indirect object pronoun placement (*Te lo doy*, *Dámelo*).

### 2.2 Error Spotting & Correction (Proofreader Mode)
* **Prompt:** *"La problema es muy grande."* or *"Yo hablas español."*
* **Student Response:** Identify and correct the error (*El problema*, *Yo hablo*).
* **Learning Objective:** Builds critical evaluation skills and targets common false friends/gender exceptions (*el agua*, *el mapa*, *la mano*).

### 2.3 Auditory Cloze (Fill-in-the-Blank Audio)
* **Prompt:** Audio sentence with a missing word or audio tone: *"Voy al supermercado para comprar _____."*
* **Student Response:** Speaks or types an appropriate missing vocabulary word.
* **Learning Objective:** Enhances context-based listening comprehension.

---

## 3. Conversational & Pragmatic Practice

### 3.1 Q&A Mini-Dialogues
* **Prompt (Audio/Text in Spanish):** *"¿Cómo te llamas?"* / *"¿Qué hora es?"* / *"¿Adónde vas los fines de semana?"*
* **Student Response (Spanish):** Speaks or writes a complete-sentence answer in Spanish.
* **Learning Objective:** Prepares students for real-world interactions using standard Spanish 1A conversational scripts.

### 3.2 Visual & Icon Prompts (Direct Image-to-Spanish Association)
* **Prompt:** Image or icon (e.g., a clock showing 3:30, a rainy weather icon, or a picture of a table).
* **Student Response:** *"Son las tres y media"* / *"Llueve"* / *"La mesa"*.
* **Learning Objective:** Removes the English translation step entirely, promoting direct visual-to-Spanish association.

---

## 4. Audio & Pronunciation Focus

### 4.1 Minimal Pair Distinctions
* **Prompt:** Audio of subtle distinctions (*pero* vs. *perro*, *esta* vs. *está*, *hablo* vs. *habló*, *año* vs. *ano*).
* **Student Response:** Selects or speaks the correct target word/meaning.
* **Learning Objective:** Trains the ear for accent marks, rolled R's, and stress placements that alter word meaning.

### 4.2 Shadowing Practice
* **Prompt:** Plays a short native Spanish sentence.
* **Student Response:** Immediately repeats it back aloud into speech-to-text; the app evaluates pronunciation cadence, speed, and accuracy.
* **Learning Objective:** Builds muscle memory for mouth/tongue positioning and natural rhythm.

---

## 5. Speed & Automaticity (Gamified Modes)

### 5.1 Pattern Completion / Analogies
* **Prompt:** *"Hablar is to hablo as Comer is to ____?"*
* **Student Response:** *"Como"*
* **Learning Objective:** Tests pattern recognition across verb families (-ar, -er, -ir).

### 5.2 Rapid-Fire Conjugation Blitz (60-Second Speed Run)
* **Prompt:** Rapid sequence of random verb + pronoun combinations.
* **Student Response:** Rapid verbal or written response to maximize correct answers within 60 seconds.
* **Learning Objective:** Builds automatic recall for high-frequency irregular verbs (*ser*, *estar*, *ir*, *tener*, *hacer*).
